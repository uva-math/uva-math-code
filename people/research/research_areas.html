Research

Faculty research interests cover the following topics: Algebraic topology, representation theory, commutative algebra and algebraic geometry, group theory, harmonic analysis, history of mathematics, low-dimensional topology, mathematical physics, PDEs, probability, operator theory, and statistics and analysis. Many faculty are supported through research grants.
