Geometry and Topology at UVa

Welcome to the home page for UVa’s geometry and topology research group. Here you’ll find information [about us](https://math.virginia.edu/geomtop/people/) and the activities we organize, including [conferences](https://math.virginia.edu/geomtop/conferences/), seminars, and reading courses. We will be hiring several postdocs in geometry/topology over the next few years, thanks to new grant support. Please see the [RTG Postocs](https://math.virginia.edu/geomtop/postdocs/) page for more details; we encourage you to apply! Many of our activities are supported by an RTG grant from the National Science Foundation (DMS-1839968).
