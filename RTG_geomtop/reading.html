Geometry/Topology Reading Courses

Each semester one or more geometry/topology faculty or postdocs organize a seminar-style reading course for graduate students. Usually these courses center around a book or collection of articles, which students study on their own and present in course meetings. Current courses are listed below; more information can be found by contacting the organizer.
