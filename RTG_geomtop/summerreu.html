Summer Undergraduate Research

The mathematics department regularly hosts undergraduates from UVa and other universities in summer undergraduate research programs, including a program focused in geometry/topology. The application deadline is in February each year; participants receive full support for housing for the 8-week program, along with a stipend. For more details on upcoming programs please see the summer research [homepage]( https://sites.google.com/view/2024-uva-topology-reu), and the program listing on [mathprograms.org](https://www.mathprograms.org/db/programs/1531).
