Affine Braid Group Action

Motivation: OεΘ\mathcal{O}_\varepsilon^\Theta has very easy categorical symmetry – the shift of grading by an element of dΛ0d\Lambda_0 sending Vermas to Vermas. Can we see that for gH{}_gH?

To WaW^{a} we can assign the corresponding affine braid group Bra\mathrm{Br}^{a}: it’s generated by TsT_{s}, s∈Ss \in S, with relations TsTtTs…⏟mst=TtTsTt…⏟mst for s≠t,mst=order⁡ of st.\underbrace{T_{s} T_{t} T_{s} \ldots}_{m_{st}}=\underbrace{T_{t} T_{s} T_{t} \ldots}_{m_{st}} \text{ for } s \neq t, \quad m_{st}=\operatorname{order} \text{ of } st. We want to construct an action of Bra\mathrm{Br}^{a} on a deformed version of HH by equivalences (of triangulated categories), which is more elementary than dealing w. gH{}_gH or HH.

Lattice and Stabilization

The embedding Λ0↪Wa\Lambda_{0} \hookrightarrow W^{a} lifts to Λ0→Bra\Lambda_{0} \rightarrow \mathrm{Br}^{a} uniquely so that for λ∈Λ0+\lambda \in \Lambda_{0}^{+} (dominant) λ↦Ttλ\lambda \mapsto T_{t_{\lambda}} (unique b/c ∀\forall element of Λ0\Lambda_{0} is the difference of dominant ones).

Notation: Jμ:=J_{\mu}:= image of μ∈Λ0\mu \in \Lambda_{0} in Br\mathrm{Br}.

Note that Δ̂(xtμ)≄Δ̂(x)*Jμ\hat{\Delta}\left(x t_{\mu}\right) \not\simeq \hat{\Delta}(x) * J_{\mu} for general x,μx, \mu. But there’s “stabilization.”

⇒T(xt−μ)−1−1J−μ=Tx−1−1⇒Δ̂(xt−μ)=Δ̂(x)*J−μ\Rightarrow T_{\left(x t_{-\mu}\right)^{-1}}^{-1} J_{-\mu} = T_{x^{-1}}^{-1} \Rightarrow \hat{\Delta}\left(x t_{-\mu}\right) = \hat{\Delta}(x) * J_{-\mu}

Lemma 1 (Definition of Δ̂st(x)\hat{\Delta}^{st}(x)). ∀x∈Wa∃μx∈Λ0+\forall x \in W^{a}\ \exists \mu_{x} \in \Lambda_{0}^{+} s.t. Δ̂(xt−μ)*Jμ\hat{\Delta}\left(x t_{-\mu}\right) * J_{\mu} doesn’t depend on μ\mu if μ≥μx\mu \geqslant \mu_{x}.
