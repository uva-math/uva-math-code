Algebraic and Geometric Representation Theory

July 17 — 21, 2026

The 16th Southeastern Lie Theory Workshop Series at University of Virginia, Charlottesville, VA

Financial support (deadline: April 25, 2026)

We will be able to provide financial support to some participants through an NSF Conference grant. Early career researchers and members of underrepresented groups in mathematics are encouraged to apply for support.

To request financial support and/or give a contributed 20-minute talk, please:

  • Register and indicate the financial support request in the registration form below. Answer the additional questions in the "NSF reporting" section. Email a copy of your most recent CV to selie.uvasat2026@gmail.com
  • The deadline for contributed talk requests is also April 25, 2026. If your talk is chosen by the organizational committee, you will be informed, together with your financial support request, in May, 2026.
The deadline to request financial support is April 25, 2026.
