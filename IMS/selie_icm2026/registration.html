Algebraic and Geometric Representation Theory

July 17 — 21, 2026

The 16th Southeastern Lie Theory Workshop Series at University of Virginia, Charlottesville, VA

Financial support (deadline: April 30, 2026)

We will be able to provide financial support to some participants through an NSF Conference grant. Early career researchers and members of underrepresented groups in mathematics are encouraged to apply for support.

To request financial support and/or give a contributed 20-minute talk, please:

  • Register and indicate the financial support request in the registration form below. Answer the additional questions in the "NSF reporting" section. Email a copy of your most recent CV to selie.uvasat2026@gmail.com
  • The deadline for contributed talk requests is also April 30, 2026. If the organizational committee chooses your talk, you will be informed, together with your financial support request, in May 2026.

Recommended conference hotel

The Department of Mathematics at the University of Virginia has negotiated special conference rates at two hotels, the Graduate by Hilton and the Courtyard by Marriott (University Medical Center), near the conference location. Please make your own reservations through the links for The Graduate Hotel and The Courtyard by Marriott. In each hotel, there are double queen and king options. Sharing a room, especially among junior participants, is encouraged. We will update the registered participants page regularly. If you would like to share a room with someone, one guest will complete the reservation in a double queen, and the room will be removed from inventory once booked. 
Please be aware of scam emails pretending to be conference organizers making hotel reservations for you. We will not be contacting participants for your payment information.

Registration form

If you plan to attend the workshop (regardless of requesting financial support), please fill out the registration form below.
  • The registration is free
  • Please be aware of phishing emails contacting you for hotel arrangements. We will not be making lodging arrangements on your behalf, collecting your credit card information, or using third-party travel agents to contact you to make hotel reservations.
