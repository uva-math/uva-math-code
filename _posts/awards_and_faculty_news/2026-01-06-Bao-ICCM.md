UVA Mathematics PhD alumnus Huanchen Bao has received three prestigious awards at the International Congress of Chinese Mathematicians (ICCM 2025), held in Shanghai, including the [ICCM Best Paper Silver Award](https://iccm.simis.cn/iccm2025/site/awards/best-paper-award) for the paper "Canonical bases arising from quantum symmetric pairs of Kac-Moody type" (Compos. Math. 157, 2021), joint with {% include person_info_just_name.html UVA_id="ww9c" %}.

Bao also received the [ICCM Silver Medal of Mathematics](https://iccm.simis.cn/iccm2025/site/awards/medal-of-mathematics) and the [ICCM Best Paper Gold Award](https://iccm.simis.cn/iccm2025/site/awards/best-paper-award) (joint with Xuhua He).

[Huanchen Bao](https://blog.nus.edu.sg/huanchen/) is currently an Associate Professor at the National University of Singapore.
