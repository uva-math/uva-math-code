Solve as many problems as you can. Full solutions on a smaller number of problems will be worth more than partial solutions on several problems.

(a) Show that ∫∑n=1∞n−2fndμ<+∞\int \sum_{n=1}^{\infty} n^{-2} f_{n} d \mu<+\infty.
(b) Use the previous part to show that for almost every x∈Xx \in X, we have that

limn→∞fn(x)n2=0\lim _{n \rightarrow \infty} \frac{f_{n}(x)}{n^{2}}=0
