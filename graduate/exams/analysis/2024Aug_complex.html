Complex Analysis General Exam August 2024

Solve as many problems as you can. Full solutions on a smaller number of problems will be worth more than partial solutions on several problems. Throughout 𝔻={z∈ℂ:|z|<1}\mathbb{D}=\{z \in \mathbb{C}:|z|<1\}. Don't use any of the Picard theorems.

Problem 1

Compute, for 0<α<10<\alpha<1,

∫0∞xαx2+4dx\int_{0}^{\infty} \frac{x^{\alpha}}{x^{2}+4} d x

You must show all estimates.
