Complex Analysis General Exam, August 2020

General guidelines: In order to receive full credit, a detailed argument (rather than a sketch of the proof) is needed. Whenever applying one of the standard theorems, please indicate that clearly.

Pledge:

Problem 1

Let ff be an entire function. Assume that

max|z|=r|f(z)|≤10log⁡r\max _{|z|=r}|f(z)| \leq 10 \log r

for r≥100r \geq 100. Shown that ff is constant.
