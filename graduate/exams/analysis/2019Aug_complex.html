Problem 1

Problem 2

Let ff be an entire function. Suppose that there is an α∈(0,∞)\alpha \in(0, \infty) and a C>0C>0 so that |f(z)|≤C|z|α|f(z)| \leq C|z|^{\alpha} for all |z|≥1|z| \geq 1. Show that ff is a polynomial.

Problem 3

Let ff be an entire function. Suppose that lim⁡z→∞f(z)=∞\lim _{z \rightarrow \infty} f(z)=\infty. Show that ff is a polynomial.
