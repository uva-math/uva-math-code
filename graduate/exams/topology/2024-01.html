Instructions: This is a four hour exam. Your solutions should be legible and clearly organized, written in complete sentences in good mathematical style. All work should be your own-no outside sources are permitted-using methods and results from the first year topology course topics. There are 8 problems; each problem is worth the same number of points.

Problem 1

Let f,g:ℝ1→ℝ2f, g: \mathbb{R}^{1} \longrightarrow \mathbb{R}^{2} be two smooth maps. Prove that the subset of ℝ1×ℝ1×ℝ2\mathbb{R}^{1} \times \mathbb{R}^{1} \times \mathbb{R}^{2} consisting of all points (x,y,z)∈ℝ1×ℝ1×ℝ2(x, y, z) \in \mathbb{R}^{1} \times \mathbb{R}^{1} \times \mathbb{R}^{2} satisfying the equation f(x)−g(x)=zf(x)-g(x)=z is a submanifold. What is its dimension?

Problem 3

Show that any smooth map f:ℝPn→ℝPnf: \mathbb{R} P^{n} \rightarrow \mathbb{R} P^{n} has a fixed point, when nn is even.

Problem 8
