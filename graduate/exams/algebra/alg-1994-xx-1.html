ALGEBRA GENERAL EXAMINATION January 15, 1994

The examination is closed book. Please write out Mour answers neatly and include your scratch work. III he allowed time is three hours; there are eight problems.

Find all the possible rational canonical forms for a 5 by 5 matrix with rational entries whose minimal polynomial is (x−2)2(x+3)(x-2)^{2}(x+3). Identify a pair having The same characteristic polynomial.
2. Prove that the Gabis group of x4−2x^{4}-2 over The rational field is isomorphic to the dihedral group of order 8.
3. Prove that y3+x2y2+x3y+xy^{3}+x^{2} y^{2}+x^{3} y+x is irreducible in ℤ[x,y]\mathbb{Z}[x, y].
4. Let GG and HH be finite groups and let f:G→Hf: G \rightarrow H be a surjective homomorphism. Let pp be a prime.
(a) Prove that if PP is a pp-Sylow subgroup of GG, then f(P)f(P) is a pp-Sylow subgroup of HH.
(b) Prove that overy pp-Sylow subgroup of HH has the form f(p)f(p) for some pp-Sylow subgroup PP of GG.
5. Give an example (and a proof that it works) of a ring RR without identity and an ideal in the direct sum R⊕RR \oplus R that does not have the form I1⊕I2I_{1} \oplus I_{2}, where I1I_{1} and I2I_{2} are ideals in RR.
6. (a) Explain why the inner automorphism group of the alternating group AnA_{n} is isomorphic to AnA_{n} for n≥4n \geq 4.
(b) Prove that for n≥3,Ann \geq 3, A_{n} has outer automorphisms.
7. In a field, it is true that if tm=1t^{m}=1 and tn=1t^{n}=1 for two relatively prime exponents mm and nn, then t=1t=1. Explain how this implies the following result for an integral domain: if am=bma^{m}=b^{m} and an=bna^{n}=b^{n} for two relatively prime positive exponents mm and nn, then a=ba=b.
8. Let RR be a commutative ring with identity and lot DD be the set of zero divisurs of RR, induding OO. Let dd be the set of ideals of RR Dat are contained in DD.
(a) Show that if MM is a maximal member of dd, then MM is a prime ideal.
(b) Use Zorn’s lemma to show that each member of DD is contained in a maximal member of dd.
(c) Conclude that DD is a union of prime ideals.
