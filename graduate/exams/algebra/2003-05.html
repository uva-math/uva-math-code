Problem 4

Show that the group G1G_{1} of all real numbers for addition is isomorphic to the group G2G_{2} of all positive real numbers for multiplication. Furthermore, show that the group H1H_{1} of all rational numbers is not isomorphic to the group H2H_{2} of all positive rational numbers for multiplication.

Problem 5

Let GG be a group such that there exists a surjective group homomorphism G→ℤG \rightarrow \mathbb{Z}. Prove that for any subgroup of finite index H⊂GH \subset G there also exists a surjective group homomorphism H→ℤH \rightarrow \mathbb{Z}.

Problem 6

Let AA be the ring of all continuous real-valued functions on [0,1][0,1]. Give an example of a maximal ideal in AA. Furthermore, give an example of an element f∈Af \in A which is not invertible, but which is not a zero divisor either.

Problem 7
