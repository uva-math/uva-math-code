ALGEBRA GENERAL EXAMINATION

January 17, 1998
Question 1 (a) What can you say about groups GG of the following orders |G||G| ? (Give reasons, making free use of any theorems you know.)
(1) |G|=24+1:G≅|G|=2^{4}+1: G \cong ?
(2) |G|=23+1:G≅|G|=2^{3}+1: G \cong ?
(b) What can you say about simple groups GG of the following orders |G||G| ? (Again, explain your answer.)
(1) |G|=2m+1:G≅|G|=2^{m}+1: G \cong ?
(2) |G|=2.3.5:G≅|G|=2.3 .5: G \cong ?
(3) |G|=22.3.5:G≅|G|=2^{2} .3 .5: G \cong ?

All your answers should be quite short.

Question 2 (a) How many non-isomorphic abelian groups are there of order 360 ?
(b) Take the free abelian group on generators x,yx, y and zz. Divide it by the relations

2x+4y+5z=06x+8y+10z=08x+12y+20z=0\begin{aligned} & 2 x+4 y+5 z=0 \\ & 6 x+8 y+10 z=0 \\ & 8 x+12 y+20 z=0 \end{aligned}

Write the resulting group as a direct sum of cyclic groups.

3.2 If a∣xa \mid x and b∣xb \mid x, then m∣xm \mid x.
