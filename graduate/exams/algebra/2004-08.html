Problem 2

( 14 points) Let GG be a group of order 56, and let PpP_{p} for p∈{2,7}p \in\{2,7\} be a Sylow pp subgroup of GG.
(a) (6 points) Show that P2P_{2} or P7P_{7} is normal in GG.

(c) ( 5 points) Show that there exists a group GG of order 56 with a non-normal P7P_{7}. You can either do this by exhibiting a concrete example with this property or by describing how to construct such a group. In the latter case you have to justify why your approach works but you needn't give all details of the construction.
