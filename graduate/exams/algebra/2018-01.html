    Problem 1

  1. ( 10 points) Classify, up to isomorphism, all finite groups of order 2p2 p, where pp is a prime number.

    2. Problem 2

  2. ( 12 points, 6 points each) Consider the ring
    R=ℤ[−11]={m+n−11∣m,n∈ℤ}R=\mathbb{Z}[\sqrt{-11}]=\{m+n \sqrt{-11} \mid m, n \in \mathbb{Z}\}.
