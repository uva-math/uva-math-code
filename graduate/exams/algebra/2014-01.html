ALGEBRA GENERAL EXAM, JANUARY 6, 2013, 9AM-1PM

Directions.

  • Show all your work and justify any statements that you make.

  • State clearly and fully any theorem you use.

  • Vague statements and hand-waving arguments will hurt your grade.

  • You may assume the statement in an earlier part proven in order to do a later part.

  • Do each problem on a separate one-sided sheet of paper, and staple them together in the correct order.
