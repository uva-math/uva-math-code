ALGEBRA GENERAL EXAMINATION

August 26, 1995

This algebra examination is closed book. Please write your answers out neatly and include all your scratch work. The time allowed is three hours, and there are seven problems altogether.

  1. Let GG be a finite group of permutations of the finite set XX. For x∈Xx \in X, let

Gx=Stab(x)={g∈G:gx=x}.G_{x}=S t a b(x)=\{g \in G: g x=x\} .

Suppose |X|=[G:Gx]|X|=\left[G: G_{x}\right] for some x∈Xx \in X. Show this holds for all x∈Xx \in X.
2. Let GG be a group of order 1001. Show:
a) GG is not simple;
b) GG is Abelian;
c) GG is cyclic.
3. Let AA be the Abelian group (written additively) generated by x1,x2,x3x_{1}, x_{2}, x_{3} satisfying the relations

4x1−2x2+4x3=07x1−8x2+x3=08x1+x2+13x3=0\begin{array}{r} 4 x_{1}-2 x_{2}+4 x_{3}=0 \\ 7 x_{1}-8 x_{2}+x_{3}=0 \\ 8 x_{1}+x_{2}+13 x_{3}=0 \end{array}

Find the order of AA.
4. Show that ℤ[10]=ℤ+ℤ10\mathbb{Z}[\sqrt{10}]=\mathbb{Z}+\mathbb{Z} \sqrt{10} is not a factorial (unique factorization) domain. Hint: show that
a) n2−10m2≠2,3n^{2}-10 m^{2} \neq 2,3 for all n,m∈ℤn, m \in \mathbb{Z}.
b) 2,3 , and 4±104 \pm \sqrt{10} are primes in ℤ[10]\mathbb{Z}[\sqrt{10}].
5. Find the Jordan canonical form of the n×nn \times n matrix A=(aij)A=\left(a_{i j}\right) with aij=1a_{i j}=1 for i≤ji \leq j and aij=0a_{i j}=0 for i>ji>j.
6. Let K/ℚK / \mathbb{Q} be a splitting field of x3−9x+12x^{3}-9 x+12. Show that there is a single normal extension L/ℚL / \mathbb{Q} in KK with ℚ≠L≠K\mathbb{Q} \neq L \neq K. Find [L:ℚ][L: \mathbb{Q}].
7. Show that every element of SO5(ℝ)\mathrm{SO}_{5}(\mathbb{R}) has a fixed point ( Ax=xA x=x for some x≠0x \neq 0 ). Recall that

SO5(ℝ)={A∈ℝ5:(Ax,Ay)=(x,y), and detA=1}S O_{5}(\mathbb{R})=\left\{A \in \mathbb{R}_{5}:(A x, A y)=(x, y), \text { and } \operatorname{det} A=1\right\}
