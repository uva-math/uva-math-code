Problem 1

(a) ( 7 pts) Let CC be a cyclic group of order n≥2n \geq 2. Explain briefly why Aut⁡(C)≅ℤn×\operatorname{Aut}(C) \cong \mathbb{Z}_{n}^{\times}(the multiplicative group of ℤn\mathbb{Z}_{n} ) and why Aut⁡(C)≅ℤn−1\operatorname{Aut}(C) \cong \mathbb{Z}_{n-1} if nn is prime.
(b) ( 7 pts) Let GG be a finite group, let pp be the smallest prime dividing |G||G|, and suppose that GG contains a normal subgroup CC of order pp. Prove that CC lies in the center of GG.
