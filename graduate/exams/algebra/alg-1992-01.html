January 1992

Closed book. Write your final answers neatly (turn in your scratch work). Three hour time limit.

8. Show that a polynomial of degree nn over a field FF has at most nn roots in FF.
3. For φ\varphi the rationals and ω\omega a primitive cube root of 1 , determine whether φ(ω32)\varphi\left(\omega^{3} \sqrt{2}\right) is a Galois extension of QQ. Give reasons.
4. Prove: If a finite group G has a normal p -Sylow subgroup Sp\mathrm{S}_{\mathrm{p}}, then φ(Sp)⊂Sp\varphi\left(\mathrm{S}_{\mathrm{p}}\right) \subset \mathrm{S}_{\mathrm{p}} for every endomorphism φ\varphi of GG.
5. A matrix EE is idempotent if E2=EE^{2}=E. Show that two idempotent matrices over a field are similar if and only if they have the same rank.
4. Prove: An integral domain has the descending chain condition on ideals if and only if it is a field.
