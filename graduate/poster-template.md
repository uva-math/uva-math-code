## Template Features

- **UVA-Branded Design**: Official blue and orange color scheme with Rotunda watermark
- **Professional Layout**: Organized sections for presenting research clearly
- **Customizable Sections**:
  - Thesis title and student information
  - Research overview and motivation
  - Key theorems, definitions, and propositions
  - Results and examples
  - References
  - Defense details (date, time, location)
  - Advisor information and student homepage

## Usage

The template uses standard LaTeX packages and can be compiled with pdfLaTeX or similar engines. Key sections to customize:

1. **Header**: Replace thesis title, student name, and defense details
2. **Content**: Update research overview, theorems, and results with your work
3. **Footer**: Add your homepage URL and advisor information
4. **References**: Include relevant citations

The template is structured using the `scrartcl` document class and includes mathematical typesetting support via `amsmath`, `amsthm`, and diagram support through `xy-pic`.
