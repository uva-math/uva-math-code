  5. Show that the series ∑n=2∞1n(ln⁡n)2\sum_{n=2}^{\infty} \frac{1}{n(\ln n)^{2}} converges.

    Solution: The series may be written ∑n=2∞f(n)\sum_{n=2}^{\infty} f(n), where f(x)=1x(ln⁡x)2f(x)=\frac{1}{x(\ln x)^{2}}.
